Our Story

NOVA Coffee was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves exceptional coffee without the pretension. Founded in 2024, we started as a small corner shop with big dreams.

Today, we've grown into a community hub where coffee lovers, remote workers, and friends gather. But our mission remains the same — serve great coffee, create meaningful connections, and make every visit memorable.

Every bean we source is ethically traded. Every cup we serve is made with care. Every customer is part of our family.
